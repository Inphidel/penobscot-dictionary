# nαnahkʷihátαke

**Part of speech:** II

**English:** it is/measures five inches (measured by five lengths of the distal phalanx of the thumb). [often used in muskrat tail divination; the longer the tail of the first muskrat taken, the greater the number of muskrats that; will be killed that season].
