# yewahkʷihátαke

**Part of speech:** II

**English:** it is/measures four inches (measured by four lengths of the distal phalanx of the thumb) [often used in muskrat tail divination; the longer the tail of the five muskrat taken, the greater the number of muskrat that will be killed that season].
