# wànəhan

**Part of speech:** II

**English:** it (object on the ground; not a house) is covered by, buried in, hidden by the snow;

## Examples

| Penobscot | English | POS |
|-----------|---------|-----|
| →wànəhan iyo ὰwəti | The road is covered with snow. |  |
| nətəmáhikan wànəhan | My axe is covered with snow. |  |
