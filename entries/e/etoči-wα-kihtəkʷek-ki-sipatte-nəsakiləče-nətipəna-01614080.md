# etońći-wőĪŐĀkiht…ôk ∑ek kiŐĀsipatte n…ôsakil…ôńćeŐĀn…ôtip…ôna

**Part of speech:** lit.

**English:** The stream was so crooked that we could indeed clasp hands (implying that the canoe had to bend, so the bowman and sternman could clasp hands).
